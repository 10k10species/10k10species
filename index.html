Thousands of animals are threatened with extinction. Every year more species are lost forever. If only we could turn back time.

That's horrible! What can I do to help?

Once a species is extinct it can never be brought back! That's why it is important to protect our endangered species. The IUCN plays a vital role in this struggle. Click the link below to find out more about them and how you can possibly help. http://support.iucnredlist.org
